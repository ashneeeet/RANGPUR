Rangpur's Hidden Gems

HISTORY

Rangpur, located in northern Bangladesh, has a rich history with roots dating back to ancient times. It was part of the kingdom of Gaur and later became an administrative region under the Mughal Empire. In the British era, Rangpur was integrated into the Rajshahi Division. Today, it is known for its agricultural productivity, particularly for crops like jute and potatoes.























CULTURE

Rangpur's culture reflects a blend of tradition and agricultural life. Known for its vibrant folk music and dance, the region celebrates festivals like Pohela Boishakh and Eid with enthusiasm.
